Welcome To Next School

Next School is one of the finest quintile-3 schools in the city.

The dedicated and committed staff seeks to supply the learners with a nurturing and caring environment during which to develop and grow into confident and compassionate young children equipped to form a positive impact on the planet.

This mission, alongside our proud tradition of innovation and academic excellence, makes us the varsity of choice for several families.

SMS is designed and conceptualized to establish new benchmarks in school level learning, experience, and education, to bring the best infrastructure and facilities With the power of technology and the internet, we bought a new online learning platform where Teachers, Parents, and Students come under a single application and check the progress of their children.
