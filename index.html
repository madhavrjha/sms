Notices For Students

7th October, 2021

Poetry Competition distributes a cumulative sum of R5,000 to winners. Persons who are 16 years old and above, and write in English. First prize is R1,000 and a book publication contract, second prize R500 and third prize R250. The top 20 prizes receive R100 each. 100 Commendable Mentions will also be published in annual anthology. Children & Teens: Persons who are between the age group of 7-15 years of age, and write in English. First prize is R250, second prize R150, and third prize R100. The top 20 prizes receive R500 each. In addition to receiving cash prizes, winners will be invited to attend prize giving event in August 2021 (subject to government travel restrictions). The winning and commended entries will also be published in the anthology.

5th October, 2021

An Inter Class Solo Singing Competition will organize on 5th October 2021. Students from class 3, 4 & 5 canparticipate in the competition. The song would be the presenter’s choice. The participants is supposed to song with a lot of composure and passion and seem to enjoy what they song. It is a tough job for the judges as they will found that voice modulation of almost all the singers was good and the pitch perfect liek last year. The Inter- Class Solo Dancing Competition was conducted on 7th Oct, 2020 for grades, 3, 4 & 5. The lively dancers set the floor on fire with their peppy presentations. Their movements captured the essence of the music and movements were, graceful and elegant. The colorful costumes of the little ones enhanced the pleasure of watching the performance. The judges made a note of the expressions, stage coverage, choreography and the applauded the effort the dancers put in.

8th May, 2021

Mother's Day is the day that we celebrate our mothers and the influence that they have on our daily lives in South Africa. Mothers and mother figures do so much for us. The day is a chance to thank them for all their hard work and make them feel appreciated. Mother's Day happens on a weekend, but you can incorporate it into your classroom easily with our wide range of resources. Why not give children the opportunity to make a card or craft to take home for the weekend? School is a great place to complete these activities, as it's bound to be a lovely surprise for mums and mother figures everywhere. Children will love the element of excitement as they secretly prepare a card or gift for their mother. We have plenty of resources to help you teach about Mother's Day in your classroom. You can easily incorporate the theme into English or maths lessons with our worksheets which are ready for you to download and print. Plus, we have a wide variety of Mother's Day card resources which are suitable for all ages to complete! Of course, we can't forget about the craft options which are simple to download and use.
